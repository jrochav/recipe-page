FEIJOADA


    INGREDIENTS

  • 250 g of Paio Frimesa
  • 250 g Frimesa Calabresa Sausage
  • 1 pack of Ingredients for Feijoada Frimesa
  • 250 g Frimesa Smoked Pork Ribs, cut into cubes
  • 250 g Frimesa Smoked Loin, cut into cubes
  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic finely chopped
  • 1 bunch of parsley, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1kg of beans

    METHOD OF PREPARATION

  1. The day before serving, soak the beans in water and store in the fridge.
  2. Cut the Frimesa salted meats and, separately, soak, changing the water at least twice.
  3. On the day of serving, blanch the Frimesa smoked meats.
  4. Put the beans and the Frimesa desalted meats to cook with plenty of water in a large pot.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the smoked meats and Frimesa sausages.
  7. Cook for a few more minutes until all meats are tender.
  8. Add the orange during cooking to remove excess fat.
  9. Remove from cooking and cut the meat and sausages into slices.
  10. Prepare a stir-fry with onion and garlic for seasoning.
  11. Add this seasoning to the beans.
  12. Return all the meats to the pan, taste the salt and pepper and let it saut√© until the beans are soft.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

Serve the feijoada with rice, breaded fried banana, farofa, cabbage, vinaigrette, pastel and orange
